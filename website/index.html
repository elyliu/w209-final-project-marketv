Introduction

You are a marketer for a newly launched TV show. You are determining where to spend your limited money to maximize your return (viewers watching your new show). The data gathered to create this visualization was generated by observing TV viewing behavior for a participating sample audidence. This includes detailed viewer-level data that can be explored by TV show. Data is available from May-June 2018.

Instructions

Select and/or type the name of a show in the Highlighted Show / Program Title dropdown.

On the Benchmarking tab, view three key metrics for each show, and compare its performance to that of other shows.

On the Show Detail tab, drill into the audience for a specific show - analyze who they are, when and how much they watch, as well as what other shows they spend their time watching.

Key Definitions:

  1. Average Viewers per Minute: The average number of viewers tuned into a show, per minute that the show was on air

  2. Total Viewers: The total number of unique viewers that tuned into a given show

  3. Viewing Minutes: The total number of minutes that viewers watched a given show
