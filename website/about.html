W209 Final Project

Summer 2018

Contributors: Ely Liu, Jennifer Podracky, Collin Reinking, Blake Williamson

Intended Audience

The intended audience for this product is marketers who are responsible for launching and evaluating the performance of new TV shows.

Goals

We want to enable users to:

  1. Understand how their show is performing relative to others

  2. Understand who is watching their show

  3. Measure and benchmark viewer engagement

  4. Understand differences in demographics and behavior between different audiences

Data Sources

The data collected and shown in this prototype is sourced from Nielsen. The data was aggregated from May and June 2018, at both the viewer- and show-level.

Overall, the dataset is approximately 2GB.

Considerations

The program titles and network names on the product prototype have been obscured for IP protection reasons.
